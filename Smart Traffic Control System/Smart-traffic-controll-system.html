Welcome

The Smart Traffic Control System is a web-based application designed to help manage road traffic efficiently in major towns and cities in Cameroon. Due to the rapid increase in vehicles and population, traffic congestion has become a major challenge, especially in cities like Douala, Yaoundé, Buea, and Limbe Etc.

Use the navigation bar to explore features.

This project uses modern technology concepts such as smart traffic signals and AI-based camera monitoring to reduce traffic delays and improve road safety. The system allows traffic officers to monitor road conditions in real time and adjust traffic signals based on traffic density.

The proposed system helps reduce long waiting times at junctions, minimize traffic jams, and improve the movement of emergency vehicles. It also provides drivers with better traffic information to choose less congested routes.

Overall, this project aims to support Cameroon’s move toward smart cities by improving traffic flow, reducing fuel wastage, and enhancing public safety on the roads.
